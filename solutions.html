Worldwide Delivery

Convenient geographical location of Latvia provides fast and economically advantageous delivery by all means of transportation (land, sea, air) to any place in the world.

Combined delivery

Combined delivery is the benchmark of KRONUS customer service! Combining different types of packaging in one shipment is not just a convenience. Principally, it means the economy of your resources because the price of such delivery is identical to the price for a carload of similar packaging.  Pallets, pallet collars, covers, fittings and used packaging can be included in combined loads.

Multi-address delivery

Multi-address delivery is a grand solution that helps to save our clients' main resources - time and money, what is always the priority for KRONUS. The opportunity to unload the delivery at multiple addresses lowers our clients' expenses on transportation. For instance, ordering a pallet load or a combined load, KRONUS can deliver a part of the load to the producer, while the other part can be unloaded at our client's warehouse.

Short leg delivery

In case you need a quick access to pallets or pallet collars, but it is impossible to keep large amounts of the empty packaging at the place of production, KRONUS will help you organise backup warehouses close to the client.
